About ScamScreener

Our Mission

ScamScreener is on a mission to protect job seekers from fraudulent employment offers. With job scams rising across the globe, we believe everyone deserves to search for work safely and confidently. We use AI and modern detection methods to screen suspicious job content and highlight risk factors clearly and quickly.

Why We Built This

Too many people have lost time, money, and personal data to fake recruiters. We created ScamScreener to give users instant tools to check offers before trusting them. Our platform brings together experience in cybersecurity, NLP, and user-centered design to provide a practical, free tool for anyone online.

What We Believe

- Everyone deserves digital safety.
- AI can empower everyday people.
- Transparency, privacy, and accuracy matter.
- The best protection is simple and accessible.
